PORTFOLIO

Since its implementation in 2015, the Mastercard Foundation Fund for Rural Prosperity has made quite an impact across Sub-Saharan Africa. In partnership with the projects it has funded, it has progressively increased financial access and literacy in rural communities. Project reports as at the end of 2017 indicate that over 400,000 rural people are accessing and using at least one of the 24 financial products and services launched or whose offering was scaled up following the Fund’s support. In addition, an estimated 215,000 people have received financial literacy training and the projects have created over 400 jobs in the countries of implementation.
