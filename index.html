CURRENT PROJECTS

Rwanda Trading Company

RTC is a private, for profit, coffee exporter, based in Kigali, Rwanda, exporting 26% of Rwanda’s annual coffee production

Prep-eez Technology Limited

Prep-eez has embarked on an innovative project to uplift smallholder cocoa farmers in Ghana

Copia Global Inc.

Copia has developed an innovative model to provide an Amazon-like service to the underserved rural BoP market

First Access

First Access aims to use data to improve financial access in emerging markets using a comprehensive agricultural credit scoring engine.

Musoni Kenya

Musoni has leveraged digital solutions to offer smallholder farmers and rural clients access to extension services such as farm inputs, farmer trainings and financial literacy training.

Inuka Africa

Inuka Africa Limited provides financial services, training and capacity building to micro, small and mid-size enterprises with a special focus on smallholder farmers and agri-business actors.
